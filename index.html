About Me

Hi, I am a fifth-year Ph. D. student at the Theory Group of EPFL, fortunate to be coadvised by Mika Göös and Ola Svensson. Prior to that, I obtained my Bachelor degree from IIIS, Tsinghua University.
I have broad interests in theoretical computer science, especially complexity theory.
